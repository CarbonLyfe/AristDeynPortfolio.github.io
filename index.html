Arist Deyn Portfolio 2026

Hello, My name is Arist Deyn! (Yes, a Pen Name I never expected to stick) I'm a comic artist currently based in Seattle Washington. Telling real stories about real characters using a hyper digital art is a contradiction in current comic storyelling I'm trying to remedy. For that reason I've turned to great, classic, human storyellers like Roy Crane, Alex Toth, Frank Miller, David Mazzuchelli, and Darwyn Cooke for inspiration. My main project is merging these artist's grounded, cinematic minimalism with Impressionist and Post impressionist fine art movements, especially the Ashcan Realists and German Expressionists.

Sequential Scene

My layouts focus on rhythmic pacing to create a sense of unfolding, trackable time. This technique helps to translate camera techniques to the page, heightening cinematic feel as both rely on a coherent sense of time that can be tracked from panel to panel.

Single Page Sequentials

Mostly demonstrating versatility of tone, while navigating humorous and serious moments, and make even the mundane compelling

Illustration

Employing a style inspired mainly by the Ashcan Realists because their approach was oriented around grounded cartoons which can be taken seriously which overlaps with comics (the medium is still cartooning afterall) and provides diverse buying option for comic fans in the now aesthetically hyper-digital market.

Thanks for looking! - Arist
