Arist Deyn Portfolio 2026

Hello, I'm Arist Deyn, a comic artist who has been published at Marvel, DC, Image, Darkhorse and IDW.
My work merges cinematic minimalism, as established by the great American Comics/Manga artists (Crane, Kirby, Toth, Tezuka, Otomo, Adachi etc.) with Impressionist and Ashcan Realist influences.
