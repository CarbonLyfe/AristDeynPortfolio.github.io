Arist Deyn Portfolio 2026

My project has been to combine the story telling focused minimalism of David Mazzuchelli, Alex Toth, and Darwyn Cooke with the Ashcan Realists. It's little known that the Ashcan artists of new york, a group of realist painters with their roots in impressionism, were the gnesis of gag cartoons and consewquently much of the style of American comics, both humorous and serious. Alex Toth prescribed a "documentarian impressionist style" perfectly coordinating with the Ashcan school.

Sequential

Rhythmic pacing to build the tension and release of scenes has become very important to me (as influenced Will Eisner and Frank Miller on DKR). Cinematic camera techniques have also become of great interest to me (as influenced by Katsuhiro Otomo and Frank Quitely as well as filmmakers like Pier Pasolini, Robert Altman, Dario Argento, and many more).

Single Pages

Combining the stle of the Ashcan realists with classic comics is of great interest to me. It's little known that the Ashcan school, a fine art movement with their roots in impressionism, realism and cartooning, was the genesis point of gag cartoons but they were also a deep influence on the style of early American comics, both humorous and serious. Alex Toth prescribed a "documentarian impressionist" style for comics, the exact mission statment of Robert Henri, the father of the Ashcan movement.
