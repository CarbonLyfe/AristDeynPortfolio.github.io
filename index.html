Arist Deyn Portfolio 2026

Hello, I'm Arist Deyn, a comic artist currently based in Seattle. My work merges cinematic minimalism with Impressionist and Ashcan Realist influences, to help me tell grounded, visually expressive stories that feel classic, cinematic and human.

Sequential Scene

My layouts focus on rhythmic pacing to create a sense of unfolding, trackable time and classic camera techniques.

Single Page Sequentials

My goal is to make every moment —humorous, serious, or ordinary— emotionally compelling, in service to the overarching narrative of the book.

Illustration

Impressionist painting methods offer a human touch that stands out in today’s hyper-digital market.
