Arist Deyn Portfolio 2026

Hello, I'm Arist Deyn, a comic artist currently based in Seattle.
My work merges cinematic minimalism with Impressionist and Ashcan Realist influences. I seek to continue blurring the line between expressive, cinematic cartooning and high art, as pioneered by Crane, Kirby, Toth and Otomo.
